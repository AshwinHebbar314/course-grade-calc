Disclaimer

This app, while it's designed to help students predict their scores as accurately as possible. We cannot and will not claim 100% accuracy of the results. This is simply because of:

  • Human error by us.
  • Mistakes in the input data from users.
  • Last moment changes in grading criteria by IIT Madras.
So, this tool is designed to only give you a good estimate of your course score and grade based on your input(s).
